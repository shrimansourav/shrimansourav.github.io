About Me

I'm Shriman Sourav Sen, a UI Designer for ESSPL, Bhubaneswar. I tackle the demand for innovative design in a field that's constantly evolving. As part of the creative team, I lead designs, produce for both online and print media, and spend time behind the camera for photo and video. My responsibilities for designing include my constantly trying to expand our reach world wide. I am always ready for projects where I can jump in and participate.

When not designing bigger and better things, I dabble with calligraphy, watch movies, listen music and enjoy putting together stellar dinner parties with friends, etc. I also try to travel to as many places possible, and try to live the local peoples life as much as I can. I am a designer, an optimist, an explorer, and a modern guy looking to take on the world one challenge at a time.

Why Me

Vision

I see digital design as an art. I'm mix different ideas, push concepts and find the best possible way to drive customer delight through sophisticated visual design.

Problem-Solver

I don't believe in design without a purpose. I believe that design plays a role in solving problems that improve the user experience. Hence, try delivering problem free designs.

Strategic

While designing stuffs, I think like the business owner and identify opportunities to grow the business or the brand, whether indirectly or directly related to design.

Hands-On

I enjoy while rolling up my sleeves and getting into the nitty gritty of design, yet my goal is ultimately to be a 'thought-leader' and provide more than ideas but inspiration.

Collaborative

I have high regards for team building, I work exceptionally well across all key functional areas including Web Design, Photography, Film Making and Customer Service.

Ambitious

I'm always looking for better opportunities and not afraid to take risks for the sake of making things better.
